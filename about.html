About Zigoti Properties

Your trusted partner in Ugandan real estate with over 15 years of excellence in property solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Zigoti Properties Uganda Limited has grown from a small local agency to one of the most respected real estate firms in the country. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide honest, professional property services that put our clients first.

Today, we manage a diverse portfolio of residential and commercial properties across Uganda, helping thousands of clients find their perfect properties while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and service.
